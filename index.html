About Us

Mahesh Pattin Souhard Sahakari Sangha Niyamit is a trusted cooperative society established to provide financial support and services to our members and community. With decades of experience in the banking sector, we offer a wide range of services including deposits, loans, and more.

Our mission is to promote financial inclusion and empower our members through reliable and customer-friendly banking services. We are committed to the economic development of our community by offering competitive interest rates and personalized financial solutions.
