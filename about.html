From the Chairman's Desk

It is my privilege and honor to address you as the Chairman of Mahesh Pattin Souhard Sahakari Sangha Ni, an institution that stands as a symbol of our Maheshwari community's unity, trust, and progressive spirit in Bagalkote district.

With your unwavering support, our cooperative society has achieved remarkable milestones:

  • 25+ crores in deposits, reflecting the confidence our members place in us.
  • 21+ crores in loans, showcasing our commitment to supporting members in their aspirations.
  • A robust membership of 1,800+ individuals, all part of our growing family.
  • A significant profit of 44+ lakhs in the previous financial year, a testament to our efficient management and collective efforts.
  • The establishment of two branches in Bagalkote and Ilkal, ensuring our services reach more members effectively.

These accomplishments highlight the strength of our cooperative model and the shared vision that drives us forward. Our Sangha has become a trusted financial partner, enabling economic growth and fostering social harmony within our community.

As we move ahead, our focus will remain on introducing innovative financial services, expanding our reach, and ensuring that every member benefits from the cooperative's growth. Together, we will continue building a brighter and more prosperous future for all.

I thank each one of you for your trust, dedication, and invaluable contributions to our journey of success.

Warm regards,

Shri Suryakant S Chindak

Chairman
